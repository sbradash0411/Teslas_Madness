H. N. A. Animal Anti-Cruelty League

Our Mission

We strive to ensure that all animals in the Chicago area have a fighting chance at finding a fur-ever home.

In thinking about what my sister would have wanted for a foundation named after her, I feel like Holly and her legacy shine through the number of animals we strive to help. She loved dogs, particularly our Aunt Gloria's spaniel, Lady (who was a puppy when Holly was little). Holly would want dogs like Lady, and cats like any of my seven fur babies, to be able to find loving homes.
