Happy Cats Make for Happy People

Posted by: Sarah Bradash

In the grand scheme of history, cats have seemed to rule over both the animal and human kingdoms. Why? According to Petfinder, calico cats are no exception; they are perceived as a feistier breed- the likes with which, I, as a calico owner, vehemently disagree.

See? All cats are sweet angels, like those pictured above and below! What say you to the nonsense of "Every cat I've ever met, has hated me?" I have been a cat mom since the tender age of toddler-hood (as I believe we had a cat when I was a baby), and cannot imagine life without the little scoundrels.

For the unfortunate folks who love animals, but suffer from allergies, I have included plenty of links to animal shelters that are local to northwest Indiana (for folks who live in the area between Chicago and South Bend) that need donations. There have recently been an influx of animals to shelters, due to the pandemic and other mitigating factors; I encourage folks who are interested to foster wherever possible.

Be sure to call all shelters to set up appointments, in the event of interest in any cats.

Most local shelters require appointments for purpose of patron and employee health and safety.
